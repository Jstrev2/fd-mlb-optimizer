⚾ DK Props Scraper

Scrapes DraftKings MLB pitcher props (earned runs, outs, Ks) and pushes to Supabase.

Step 1: Drag this to your bookmarks bar

📥 Scrape DK Props

↑ Drag this green button to your bookmarks bar (or right-click → Bookmark This Link)

Step 2: Navigate to DK props pages

Open these pages one at a time and click the bookmarklet on each:

  • Pitcher Earned Runs
  • Pitcher Outs Recorded
  • Pitcher Walks Allowed
  • Pitcher Hits Allowed

Each click scrapes the page and pushes to Supabase. Then hit Import in the optimizer app.

How it works

  1. You open DK in your browser (residential IP = no block)
  2. Click the bookmarklet — it reads the props from the page DOM
  3. Pushes the data to Supabase dk_props table
  4. Our import script picks up the DK data and merges it with FD props
